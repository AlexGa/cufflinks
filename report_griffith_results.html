Griffith et al

In Griffith et al, the authors introduce ALEXA-Seq, a software platform for analyzing gene expression and with support for measuring levels of individual splicing events. As part of that study, the authors generating a large qPCR data set with probes that target alternative splice variants of many genes. This section shows Cuffdiff's output compared to the Griffith qPCR data.

In the above plot, isoform-level log fold changes obtained by RNA-Seq (using Cuffdiff 2) are compared against those obtained qPCR. Each point represents an isoform-specific splicing event.
